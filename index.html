About me

Hello! I am Monica, a web developer located currently in the Greater Seattle Area. I love building web applications and working with code.

I've been playing with code off and on since I was a little girl working on a TI-994A, programming with chars to make figures on the screen come alive. Most recently, I've designed user navigation through a site for Livelygig, where contractors get connected with clients, and built parts of a web application using React.js, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS for use in digital signage for cruise ships sailing the seas.

Programming for me holds a kind of special magic, a sense of the unknown, and a sense of the familiar. It allows me to explore the world, to encounter many different ideas and ways of thinking, and to work through all manner of interesting problems every day. I am a problem solver by nature, and it thrills me when I get to tackle a new set presented by yet another twist in the great saga that is the coding adventure.
